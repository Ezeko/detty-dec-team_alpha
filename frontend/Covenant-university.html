Overview

Covenant University is a growing, dynamic vision-birthed, vision driven University, founded on Christian mission ethos and committed to pioneering excellence at the cutting edge of learning. We are driven by the compelling vision of raising a new generation of leaders for the African continent on the platform of Holistic, Human Development and integrated learaning curriculum, in order to raise Total Men who will go out to develop the world. Our Core-Values of Spirituality, Possibility Mentality, Capacity Building, Integrity, Responsibility, Diligence and Sacrificeare what defines our commitment to excellence.
